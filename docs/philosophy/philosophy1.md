# Inkblot Philosophy

Inkblot is a living mythology platform designed to enable creators to collaboratively evolve complex universes through interconnected narratives, competing factions, and evolving timelines.

## Core Principles

- **Living Narratives**: Stories aren't static—they evolve through collaboration and branching timelines
- **Canon Governance**: Community-driven consensus on what becomes official lore
- **Narrative Depth**: Support for shadow narratives, alternate timelines, and competing perspectives
- **Collaborative Creation**: Multiple creators contribute to a shared universe

See [shadow-theory.md](./shadow-theory.md) for advanced narrative concepts.
